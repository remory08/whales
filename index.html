Whales: Magnificent Mammals

  • There are many species of whales.
  • There are two types of whales- baleen and toothed whales.
  • Whales are mammals and the Blue Whale is the largest mammal on Earth.
  • Whales communicate using lyrical sounds.
  • When a whale slaps its tale, it is alerting others of danger.
  • Whales jump out of the water!
  • Whales have blubber to keep them warm.
